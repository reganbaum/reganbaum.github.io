Hi, I'm Regan.

My story begins in Ohio. Born and raised, I could not wait to escape the Midwest. When college came, I went off to North Carolina. While in college, I had the opportunity to travel to Tanzania, where I learned more about participatory learning. Then, I made it to Australia, where I discovered my passion for designing and developing simple user experiences. Before coming back to North Carolina, I made a stop in Silicon Valley to explore the tech scene on the West Coast.

Two years of traveling around the world has taught me a lot about myself and the world. I have gained an appreciation for Ohio and where I come from. I have discovered new cultures, ideas, and passions, but I still have so much left to discover. I can't wait to see where my next adventure takes me.

Want to find out more? Scroll down to check out what I've been up to, or feel free to email me or connect on LinkedIn. I'd love to chat.
